About the Project

How It Works

  • 📤 Upload four NIfTI MRI files (FLAIR, T2, T1CE, T1)
  • 🧠 AI model segments tumor regions
  • 🖼️ View slices across Axial, Coronal, and Sagittal planes
  • 🎞️ Generate and watch 3D segmentation videos
  • 📊 Analyze tumor volumes by type (ET, TC, WT)
  • 💾 Download images, videos, and segmentation mask

Key Features

📤 Multi-modal Support

Handles FLAIR, T2, T1CE, and T1 MRI inputs.

🖼️ Real-time Slice Viewer

Explore axial, coronal, and sagittal views.

🎞️ 3D Segmentation Videos

Generate videos across modalities and views.

📊 Volume Analysis

Get percentage breakdowns of tumor types.

💾 Downloadable Results

Export segmentation masks and visual data.

⚙️ Flask + TensorFlow

Backend powered by deep learning and Flask API.
